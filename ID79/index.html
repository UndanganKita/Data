Our Love Story

At the beginning of our acquaintance, we were in the same class when we were in college. He sent a private chat because he knew that we were from the same city. From there we got to know each other.


After getting closer when we met at the Chancellor’s Cup, we started communicating and meeting up frequently. Then he expressed his feelings and said he wanted to commit.

Year after year passed and brought us closer, we became convinced that we were meant to be together. It wasn’t easy for us to get to this point, but it was all worth it.Happy marriages begin when we marry the ones we love, and they blossom when we love the ones we marry.
