Our Love Story

First Meeting

It was in 2020, when we met each other. We went to the same college and had some mutual friends.

As we got to know closer each other, we felt ‘click’ and decided to have an official relationship in 2021.

Nevertheless, our relationship was not always having a good story. We went through ups and downs.

Two Become One

3 years seemed so fast, and through a rollercoaster journey, finally, in 2023, by the end of December, we decided to have a more serious relationship.

Long story short, in the midst of our marriage preparations, Raymond gave Rosa a little proposal surprise, and YES ! We were officially engaged in Bali !

New Journey

Then we celebrated our Sangjit day in June 2024, together with our family and close friends.

Now we’re very excited to counting down our Big Day in August. It’s getting real. A new milestone, towards our forever.
